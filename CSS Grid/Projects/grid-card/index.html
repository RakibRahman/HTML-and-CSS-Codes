Miso udon noodle soup

Miso udon noodle soup is a comforting dish of thick wheat noodles simmered in a savory broth with meat, tofu, and vegetables. This soothing variation that includes sake in the broth is like a warm hug in a bowl.

Sally's Apizza

Sally's Apizza is a New Haven classic, operating from the same location where they opened in the late 1930s in New Haven's Wooster Square. Their pizza is traditionally thin-crust, topped with tomato sauce, garlic and "mozz."

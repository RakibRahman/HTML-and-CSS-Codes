Descendant Selector

article h3
The article h3 selector is a descendant selector, only selecting h3 elements that fall inside of an article element.

Direct Child Selector

article p
article > p is a direct child selector only identifying p elements that fall directly within an article element. Any p element placed outside of an article element, or nested inside of another element other than the article element, will not be selected.
